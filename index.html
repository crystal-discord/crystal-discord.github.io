Crystal:
Crystal is a multi-functional discord bot. It has music commands, moderation commands to kick/ban members and some other miscellaneous commands. It also has utility commands and some fun commands, including reddit and global news command.
The bot was partially written by Hisham (malok. on discord), and its community manager is BitLlama#1733. The template for the bot is galnir's Master-Bot, which is licensed under the GNU GPL v3.0 license.
